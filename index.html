About

Welcome! I'm Francisco, a Msc astronomy student in U. de Chile. As you might expect, I spent most of my time thinking about how to understand the universe and writing code.

I'm part of the Arctomo group, guided by professor Sebastián López. I love teaching and science comunication, as I believe science needs to be shared with everyone and it is also my way of returning something to the community.

Feel free explore my early scientific career trying to understand our universe in different fronts, from the chemistry of planet forming enviroments to exploring extragalactic enviroments after a few billion years from the Big Bang.
